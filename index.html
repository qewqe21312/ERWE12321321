Squirrel Away is a handcrafted precision 2D platformer where you step up to help a squirrel couple to find acorn treasures from the mystic island based on a fabulistic world.


In this tense treasure heist adventure you will face the following; A utopian island with eye-catching terrains, unsolved mysteries, and religious cultic inhabitants who worship the mighty golden acorn. Wast amounts of handmade & hardcore levels with a huge variety of enemies and obstacles to stop you from disturbing the balance of the island. The return of some unique and forgotten gameplay elements to check your reflexes and nerves. Heist of the mighty golden acorn!
